About

Designing for Change

The application features a user-friendly carbon footprint calculator that evaluates daily activities, travel, energy consumption, and dietary choices. Users can establish personal sustainability goals and engage in challenges, promoting motivation and a sense of achievement. A comprehensive resource hub offers tips and guides on adopting sustainable practices, fostering a community through social connectivity. Leveraging machine learning, the app delivers personalized recommendations for reducing carbon footprints, while users can track their progress over time, visualizing the environmental impact of their sustainable actions. The holistic approach encourages a greener lifestyle with a focus on individual empowerment and community engagement.
